## **Core Insight**  
Digital twins and behavioral models increasingly rely on **personality archetypes** (e.g., "Type A", "Risk-Averse"). This paper shows why these fail:  

| Archetype Approach | Psycho-Digital Mapping |
|--------------------|------------------------|
| Classifies people  | Analyzes systems       |
| Assumes fixed traits | Maps dynamic constraints |
| Asks "Who are you?" | Asks "What are you seeing?" |
| Predicts behavior  | Reveals steering mechanisms |
